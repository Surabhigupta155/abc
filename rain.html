LOVE

Love is a journey that only few lucky people have a chance to be in...

Love is something which makes you feel like you have everything even when you don't have anything...

Love is only once a lifetime experience. If it happens twice, either of the one wasn't love...

When you are in love, you can actually feel the other person.. You can feel their pain, and it seems like you are also in that pain...

Love is an emotion to never give up on an the other person, how bad the situation might even be...

Love is strength that makes you feel special and motivated all the time...

Being away from you love makes you very sad.. But on the other hand you feel happy that you have someone everytime by your side even you are far away from each other...

In this world full of fake people finding someone who genuinely loves you is a blessing to you...

Love is never because of money, physical appearance, or any other materialistic thing...

It is just a very strong emotion which cannot be explained in words because it's that powerful...

Love can never be defined by anyone, it can only be felt and experienced
